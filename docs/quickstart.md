## Getting Started

Before you begin, make sure you have the following:

-   [A NEAR wallet](https://wallet.near.org)
-   Access to the [NEAR Treasury application](https://near.social/treasury-factory.near/widget/app?page=create-treasury)


## Installation and Setup

1.  Visit the [NEAR Treasury](https://near.social/treasury-factory.near/widget/app?page=create-treasury) web app and login with your NEAR account.
    
:::info
To create a Treasury, you'll need to have at least 9 NEAR available to cover the data storage deposit for both Sputnik DAO and the frontend hosting.
:::

### Starting the wizard

2.  Follow the on-screen instructions to create your NEAR Treasury.

<center>
<img src="/img/create/treasury-creation-1.png" width="60%" />
</center>

### Setting the Treasury details

3. Pick a name for your application account.

_(e.g., `my-near-treasury`)_

<center>
<img src="/img/create/treasury-creation-2.png" width="60%" />
</center>

3. Set your DAO display name.

_(e.g., `My Near Treasury`)_

<center>
<img src="/img/create/treasury-creation-3.png" width="60%" />
</center>

### Adding Team Members

4.  Select team members and set their permissions.

<center>
<img src="/img/create/treasury-creation-4.png" width="60%" />
</center>

### Review and Confirm

5. Check the Summary and confirm your data.

![creation](/img/create/treasury-creation-5.png)

6. Confirm the transaction (deposit 9 NEAR)

<center>
<img src="/img/create/treasury-creation-6.png" width="80%" />
</center>

### Ready to go

7. Congratulations :tada: Your treasury is ready!
